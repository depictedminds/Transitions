Our Specials

Current specials of the day:

Cheese on toast

A lovingly prepared slice of cheese, on a piece of bread heated to the precise temperature to turn it into toast.
Served together on a plate.

Serves 1.

Cheese (sans-toast)

A lovingly prepared slice of cheese, on a piece of bread heated to the precise temperature to turn it into toast.

Said toast is then removed, fed to the local wildlife, and slightly warm cheese is placed directly onto plate for your enjoyment
Served on a plate. Note, cheese may stick to plate.

Serves 1, and some wildlife.

Smoked Pork Jowl with Pickles

Slowly smoked cured pork jowl over pecan wood and served with thin slices of the luscious meat as an appetizer with as many pickled ingredients as possible, including house-made half-sour pickles, pickled chiles and pickled mustard seeds stirred into the blackstrap-molasses garnish.
Seasonal requirements may result in said meat, pickled ingredients, and garnish being replaced with a slice of cheese.

Serves 1 (pending cheese availability)
